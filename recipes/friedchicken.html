Fried Chicken

Description

This is about as southern as it gets. Classic fried chicken is best served along side ketchup and french fries. It may be greasy, but it sure is tasty!

Ingredients

  • Chicken (whatever part you like most)
  • Bread crumbs
  • Eggs
  • Cajun seasoning
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Prepare stirred eggs in one bowl, chicken on one plate, and breadcrumbs on one last plate.
  2. Put chicken in eggs, then cover in breadcrumbs
  3. Fry the chicken in a pan using cooking oil
  4. Put desired amount of cajun seasoning and salt on chicken while it is cooking.
  5. Check middle to make sure the chicken is cooked all the way through and serve it on your plate.
