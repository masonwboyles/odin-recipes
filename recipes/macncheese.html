Mac n Cheese

Description

This is a southern home classic. Made by grandmas across the globe, it is best served along with ham and mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • Uncooked macacroni pasta
  • Chedder cheese
  • Milk
  • Bread crumbs
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Begin boiling water
  2. Once water is boiling, put the pasta into the water for 10 minutes or until fully cooked
  3. Strain water from pot and mix the chedder and splash of milk with the pasta.
  4. Finally sprinkle breadcrumbs on top of macaroni and bake until cooked fully.
