Pizza

Description

This is a recipe for the finest new york style pizza. It uses the most classic ingredients and is perfect for those looking to make some classic new york style pizzas like the real Italians!

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Provolone
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomatoe paste
  • Basil
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Leave the dough out to rise. This takes about 6-8 hours to to properly, so plan ahead!
  2. Combine pepper, basil, 1 can tomato paste, and 1 can tomato sauce and stir well. This will create your sauce.
  3. Flatten the dough on a pizza pan into a circle and place it in the oven for about 5 minutes or until the dough becomes noticeably different. (More golden brown).
  4. Take the dough out and spread sauce evenly and then spread mozzerella and provolone on top.
  5. Return the pizza to the oven for another 10 minutes or so and take it out when its cooked all the way through.
